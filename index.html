Lovely Oven

Our Philosophy

Fire, Soul &
Honest Ingredients

🔥

The Grill

We cook over charcoal the way grandmothers did — slowly, patiently, letting the fire do its work. The smoke is the seasoning you can't fake.

🌿

The Herbs

Rau thơm, lemongrass, laksa leaf, perilla — our herb garden is harvested each morning. Fresh is not optional. It's the whole point.

🏮

The Warmth

A meal at Lovely Oven is never just food. It's a table laid with care, a bowl delivered with pride, a moment shared with family.
